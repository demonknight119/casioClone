G-SHOCK DEVELOPMENT STORY

G-SHOCK was born of an unwavering determination to overturn the prevailing view that watches were fragile things that easily break.
From this G-SHOCK toughness comes a line of watches slightly smaller than the originals.
These more compact watches are designed to bring the joy of a custom-fit lifestyle to even more people.
